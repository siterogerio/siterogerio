About Rogério Oliveira

Rogério is a pioneer for the massification of telecommunications platforms in Brazil. Still in the 90's, his return of experience and studies in the USA provoked a revolution in the technology supply market for this purpose in Brazil, because the country at that time was undergoing the biggest transformation of its networks of the last 50 years, very much, New technologies were needed to increase mass economy.

Rogério planned and made possible the entry of AVAYA, POLYCOM, PANASONIC (in all these companies Rogério was awarded as a leader in terms of project level and sales volume), among many other global companies for the newly established massive telecommunications market. In the first decade of operation more than 100 million users on the network, enabling banks, government systems, health systems, among others with total need due to their capillarity could be integrated.

This golden age of communications in Brazil has Rogério Oliveira in the corporate business segment as one of its main leaders. It created a market, expanded its distribution and made it possible to provide services in a country with continental dimensions, which until then were not possible due to the absence of these products in Brazil.

It is important to note that Brazil is still a country totally dependent on technology imports and to this day the companies idealized by Rogério are the gateway to much of this large international
industry.

Rogério is currently CEO at OLIBRAS TECNOLOGIA LTDA, Director and board member of OLITEL BRASIL TELECOMUNICAÇÕES S / A and Managing Partner of OLT TEHC LLC in the United States of America.
